How the quoting system works

Quote, save, share, book, and track every signal.

The calculator is now a lightweight quote portal: save history, reopen past lanes, share tracked quote links, and send booking requests with the shipment details already organized.

1Create a quote

Choose lane mode, city, service level, dimensions, fuel, tax, and accessorials. The total updates live from the current lane rate table.

2Save or share

Save creates a cloud quote and tracked share URL. Anonymous saves can later be claimed after login.

3Book

Booking sends the request to One Kind Express with the contact details, lane, service level, and pricing summary included.

Live intelligence

Fuel, weather, route, and item capture.

The Intel tab uses public diesel data and route weather to recommend fuel surcharge and handling upgrades. The photo upload can pre-fill item type, dimensions, and weight estimates. Photo-based measurements are estimates unless the image contains a label, scale, or known reference object.

Recommended workflow

  1. Build the quote and check the Breakdown tab for billable weight, fuel, tax, and total.
  2. Use Apply live fuel if the diesel index suggests a fuel surcharge.
  3. Open Journey to review pickup, line-haul, and final-mile operational checkpoints.
  4. Use Compare to pick the best service level and explain the value difference.
  5. Save the quote before sharing. The customer can return from Dashboard or the share link.
  6. When the customer is ready, press Request booking and confirm contact details.

FAQ

Do customers need passwords?

No. Magic link creates and verifies the account. Google sign-in can also be used when enabled.

What happens after I request booking?

Your quote, contact details, lane, selected service, item details, and notes are sent to One Kind Express for follow-up.

Can customers find old quotes?

Yes. Signed-in customers use the Client Dashboard. Anonymous customers can use the share URL; after login, anonymous browser quotes can be claimed.

Are photo measurements exact?

No image-only estimate can guarantee exact dimensions from a normal photo. It gives useful freight estimates; final booking should confirm packaged dimensions and weight.

Can I compare service levels?

Yes. The Compare tab shows the price difference between service levels so you can choose the right mix of cost and handling.
