    From rough request to booking-ready quote.

    Use this flow when a prospect sends photos, a purchase order, or a loose list of furniture. It keeps the quote fast, but still reviewable.

    1
    Capture the shipment.Upload product photos, label photos, order screenshots, or a PDF order. AI drafts item rows and explains what it used.
    2
    Review every row.Confirm quantity, item type, packaged dimensions, and total weight. If a label or order line has exact data, keep that over a visual estimate.
    3
    Pick the service level.Use Compare to decide whether curbside is enough or if Threshold, Room of Choice, or White Glove better matches the freight.
    4

    5

    faq

    Common questions before sending a quote.

    Does AI change the price by itself?No. AI fills editable item rows. Pricing still comes from the lane, service level, weight, cube, pieces, fuel, accessorials, and tax.
    Can I upload a PDF order?Yes. Use a PDF invoice, quote sheet, packing list, or product order. If dimensions or quantities are printed there, AI should prefer them.
    What if multiple identical chairs appear?Identical items should be grouped into one row using Same items. Different products stay separated so handling is clear.
    Same items or pieces per item?Use Same items for repeated identical units, like 5 TVs. Use Pieces / item for a set with several handling pieces, like one bedroom set with 5 pieces.
    What if freight is over 10 linear feet?The calculator pauses instant pricing and sends the shipment to OKE for a custom review because equipment, carrier capacity, and lane rules need confirmation.
    Why does the AI explain itself?The explanation tells you whether it used visible label data, an order line, or a visual freight estimate, so you know what to trust.
    What should I verify before booking?Origin, destination, service level, total pieces, dimensions, weight, pickup date, access notes, and contact details.
    What does the share link do?It opens the quote read-only so the customer can return, review, and confirm booking without rebuilding the calculator.
